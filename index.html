Welcome to Accra - a city of life, energy and culture

Named “Africa's capital of cool” by the New York Times, Accra is known for its colorful textiles, contemporary art, popular nightlife and entertainment scenes. Accra is the economic,political, and communications center of Ghana. Visitors often describe the city as safe and welcoming. People from around the world visit every year Accra for Detty December, Afrochella, and the Chale Wote Street Art Festival.
